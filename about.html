Welcome to Sazish Academy!

Learn to Code! With the world's largest web developer site.

Sazish Academy is a non-profit educational organization that provides free online learning resources across a wide range of subjects. It was founded in 2006 by Salman Khan with the goal of offering a high-quality education to anyone, anywhere. The platform offers a vast collection of educational materials, including video lessons, practice exercises, quizzes, and interactive tools. Khan Academy covers subjects such as math, science, computer programming, history, art, economics, and more ...
